How It Works

Set up your surround sound system in just a few simple steps

1

Scan & Discover

The app automatically scans for nearby Bluetooth speakers and displays them in an easy-to-use interface.

2

Connect & Assign

Connect to multiple speakers simultaneously and assign each one to a specific audio channel in your surround setup.

3

Configure & Test

Use built-in test tones to position speakers correctly and fine-tune volume levels for each channel.

4

Enjoy Surround Sound

Play your favorite music, movies, or games and experience immersive surround sound from your mobile device.
